ABOUT ME

Who am I

I was one of those “Lego Kids” growing up – always building things. After realizing I couldn’t play Legos my entire life, I attended the University of Dayton, where I studied Marketing, not because everyone did, but because I was fascinated with the challenge of connecting products with people. In the summer of 2013, I interned with one of the top-15 accelerators in the country – Jumpstart Foundry. In this internship, I watched the process of Software Engineers turning ideas into amazing applications, reigniting my love of building something from nothing, and leading me to join the eighth cohort at NSS. I can’t wait to take what i've learned in these last six months and continue my love of building things, for a long time to come.
